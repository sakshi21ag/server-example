In a world where we do not permit ourselves to breathe and are constantly surrounded by technology in the hustle and bustle of our chaotic lives, we simply forget to live in the present moment.

Thus, Pause provides people with different strategies to be fully present in the moment and slow down their busy lives. It focusses on giving us a break from our rigorous lives, even if it is for 2 minutes. It helps to declutter the mind and have a different and refreshed perspective and attitude to our busy lives and to reflect on ourselves.

You can choose to create your own personalized book for customized tactics and strategies that you want to participate and perform in order to relax and pause. In addition, you can also share and submit your own secret tactics with everyone else to perform, that helps you to destress and relax.
