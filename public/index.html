In a world where we do not permit ourselves to breathe and are constantly surrounded by technology in the hustle and bustle of our chaotic lives, we simply forget to live in the present moment.

Thus, Pause. provides people with different strategies to be fully present and slow down our busy lives. It focusses us to take a break from our rigorous lives even if that is for 2 minutes.

So stop stressing and calm down.
